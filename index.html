Rules

Read carefully before using LuaLand.RSL

No Sharing Invalid Script — No sharing any invalid or not working scripts in any tabs. Unless the script actually has bugs.

No Account Hacking — Hacking accounts here isn't allowed. You will get a permanent account ban.

No Comments Spam — Spamming comments to a post will cause permanent bans, like repeated messages without delay.

No Post Spam — Spamming or repeating uploading a post isn't allowed unless you have the verify badge.

No Code Cracking — Deobfuscating client scripts will result in a permanent ban by developers.

No Malicious Files — Uploading malicious or unverified files in executors tab isn't allowed. You must verify yourself.

No Dislike Spam — Spam disliking a user post may cause a permanent ban.

Report Immediately — Please report immediately the users who break the rules in this website.

Give the Real Script Creator — In uploading a script, give the real creator. If unknown, type "UNKNOWN".

No Multiple Accounts — Do not create 10 or more accounts.

No Sharing Personal Info — Do not share passwords, protect.me passwords, or other personal info.

These are the rules! Click the arrow to navigate to next page!

How to get Verify Badge?

To get the verify badge here are the steps:

Make sure your account has 50 or more followers.

Make sure you are connected to our Discord server.

Make sure you have 25 posts or more, with 50 or more likes.

Make sure your account has a thumbnail (avatar).

Make sure you follow our rules and understand the topics.

Verified Badge looks like this:

Verified

This badge appears beside your username when you meet all requirements and get approved by a developer.
